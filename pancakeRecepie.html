Pancake recepie Swedish meatballs recepie

Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 2 cups biscuit/baking mix
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup marched ripe bananas
  • 3/4 cup swirled milk choclate and peanut butter chips
  • maple syrup and additional swirled milk choclate and peanut butter chips, optional

Direction

  • Place biscuit mix in a large bowl. whisk the egg, milk
    and bananas; stir into biscuit mix just until moistened. stir in chips
  • Pour batter by 1/4 cupfuls onto a greased hot griddle;
    turn when bubbles form on top. Cook until the second side is golden brown.
    Serve with syrup and additional chips if desired. Yield: 12 pancakes.

Nutritional information

  • 589 calories
  • 25g fat (16g saturated fat)
  • 59mg cholesterol
  • 903mg sodium
  • 74g carbohydrate (29g sugars, 3g fiber)
  • 17g protein
